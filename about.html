Vincent Musser is an independent contractor in the Harrisburg/Carlisle area of Pennsylvania with 20 years of experience in custom residential remodeling. He began his career and learned the trade under the guidance of his father, an accomplished builder\remodeler.

From concept to execution, Vincent maintains attention to detail to ensure the job is done right. He drafts clearly-worded contracts to explain exactly how work will be completed, keeps workspaces neat and clean, and delivers a quality end product.

A craftsman first and foremost, Vincent's first priority is quality. Creating, restoring, and problem-solving are processes he enjoys and takes pride in.
